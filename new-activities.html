The New Four

Discover Your Sport

Each program is led by a qualified coach and open to beginners and experienced athletes alike.

New Sport

Wrestling

Wrestling is one of the oldest competitive sports in the world. Our program focuses on freestyle and Greco-Roman techniques, building explosive strength, core stability, and tactical grappling skills. Sessions are open to all fitness levels — no prior experience required.

📅 Schedule: Mon & Wed, 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM 📍 Venue: Sports Hall B 👤 Coach: Sensei Kofi Agyei 💰 Fee: GH₵ 80 / semester

New Sport

Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art emphasising fast kicks, agility, and mental discipline. Our World Taekwondo–certified instructor delivers structured belt progression from white belt upward. Community members are welcome alongside university students.

📅 Schedule: Tue & Thu, 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM 📍 Venue: Multipurpose Hall 👤 Coach: Master Ama Boateng 💰 Fee: GH₵ 90 / semester
Book a Trial Session
🥋
New Sport

Judo

Judo — "the gentle way" — teaches participants to use an opponent's force against them through throws, holds, and ground control. Our Judo program follows the IJF curriculum and is ideal for students interested in self-defence, discipline, and competition.

📅 Schedule: Sat, 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM 📍 Venue: Martial Arts Dojo 👤 Coach: Sensei Yaw Darko 💰 Fee: GH₵ 85 / semester
Book a Trial Session
🏐
New Sport

Volleyball

Both indoor and beach volleyball are offered. The program caters to beginners learning serve-and-pass fundamentals as well as experienced players looking to join our competitive mixed league. Great for teamwork, communication, and cardiovascular fitness.

📅 Schedule: Fri, 3:00 PM – 5:30 PM 📍 Venue: Outdoor Courts / Gym 👤 Coach: Coach Priscilla Tetteh 💰 Fee: GH₵ 75 / semester
Book a Trial Session
