Who We Are

About the Academy

Built by students, for students. Backed by the SRC and community partners to foster sporting excellence at Pentecost University.

Our Story

Empowering Athletes Since 2019

The SRC Sports Academy was founded as an initiative of the Student Representative Council of Pentecost University to channel student energy, promote healthy lifestyles, and build national-level sporting talent from within the university community.

What began with football and table tennis has grown into a full 10-sport institution with qualified coaches, dedicated facilities, and a growing regional reputation.

In 2025, thanks to new funding secured from national sports bodies and community partners, the Academy expanded with four new disciplines: Wrestling, Taekwondo, Judo, and Volleyball.

Funding & Partners

How We're Funded

Transparent about our support. The Academy operates through a combination of institutional support and community partnerships.

🏫 Pentecost University (SRC)

Core funding from the university's Student Representative Council budget, allocated annually for sports development and facility maintenance.

🏅 Ghana Sports Authority

Grant funding from the national sports authority to support grassroots development of wrestling, taekwondo, and judo programs.

🤝 Community Partners

Donations and equipment contributions from local businesses, alumni, and community sports bodies who believe in the Academy's mission.

💳 Membership Fees

Nominal annual membership fees from registered students and community participants contribute to day-to-day running costs.

🏆 Tournament Revenue

Entry fees from inter-university tournaments and community leagues hosted by the Academy provide supplementary income.

📢 Sponsorships (Upcoming)

We are actively seeking corporate sponsorships to fund facility upgrades, travel to national competitions, and online payment integration.
